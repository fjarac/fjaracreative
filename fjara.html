About

Fjara Creative is the home of the design and illustration work of Hilary Tricker, a British designer currently based in Iceland. She has been interested in art and design from a young age and spent her first Saturday job paycheck on a Wacom tablet.

“Fjara” is an Icelandic word for beach. Hilary grew up near the sea in the UK and now happily lives right next to one of southern Iceland’s black volcanic beaches.

Services

Fjara Creative offers a range of graphics, design, and illustration services.

  • Digital elements such as logos, website graphics, and social media banners.
  • Designs suitable for printing including flyers, business cards, and posters.
  • Illustrations and custom artwork.

Please get in touch for more details.
